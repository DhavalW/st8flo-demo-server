So, you went through 6 - 10,000 lines of text based code ( in short : a novel ),


filled to the bone with sloppy formatting, terrible indentation,
& impossibly annoying naming conventions,

passed on to you by devs, who almost fanatically believed
that sloppy code was the new black





Wasted countless hours, constructing a mental flowchart of it,


& probably several more, just beating the sheer fatigue that set in

while going through it line-by-line, function-by-function,

even in the face of a tight deadline,





All, just to find that ONE, retarded, but critical bug,


hidden deep within the code somewhere.





& NOW you need to repeat it again !


for some 100 odd, remaining bugs...


Sound familiar ?


frustratingly perhaps ?

enough to maybe try a better approach ?
